Member or Not, You Don’t Want to Miss the
District 35 Toastmasters 2022 Conference

District 35 is holding its first hybrid Spring Conference on May 7th and May 14th.
The 2022 District 35 Spring Conference is a hybrid event, meaning some people will attend in person on May
14th at Hotel Marshfield, and some people will attend the conference remotely, online.

The District 35 Table Topics and International Speech Contests will be held online only on May 7th. During the
days in between, there will be some other online events, so tune back to the district website often for details.

You have the opportunity to register as an Online Attendee or as an In-person Attendee. In-person Attendees
will also have access to online events such as the online speech contests.

Zoom links will be sent to all registered attendees prior to the May 7th District Table Topics and International During the
Speech Contests whether you have registered to attend the conference on May 14th online or in-person. The
speech contests are online only.

2022 Conference Costs

IN-PERSON REGISTRATION
WITH REGISTERED CLUB
In-person Registration includes the option for your Toastmasters Club to pay a
nominal fee for all the club members to attend the conference events at no cost
except for the cost of individually purchased meals.
• Club Registration before April 11 - $100
• Club Registration from April 11 – May 7 - $125
If your club is registering, make sure it is registered before April 10th. Individual
members must wait to register until after the club is registered to register
themselves without incurring additional registration costs.
• Club Member - Registered Club - before April 11 - $50 - Includes lunch and
dinner on May 14th
• Club Member - Registered Club - from April 11- May 7 - $60 - Includes lunch
and dinner on May 14th

IN-PERSON REGISTRATION
WITHOUT REGISTERED CLUB
• Club Member - before April 11 - $100 - Includes all Events and Lunch and
Dinner on May 14th
• Club Member - from April 11- May 7 - $120 - Includes all Events and Lunch
and Dinner on May 14th

GUEST REGISTRATION - MEALS ONLY
Guest Lunch - before April 11 - $25
• Guest Lunch - from May 7 - April 30 - $30
• Guest Dinner - before April 11 - $35
• Guest Dinner - from May 7 - April 30 - $40
Guest meals include functions such as Awards and Recognition, Happy Hour,
and Hall of Fame

ONLINE REGISTRATION
• Online Registration - Registered Club: Includes Education Sessions and
Contests - $0
• Online registration - Club Not Registered: Includes Education Sessions and
Contests - $25.00
Note: While we try to provide the same opportunities for online and in-person
attendees, technological and cost limitations cannot guarantee that all events will
be available or offer the same experience to online attendees. If possible to safely
attend in person, you may want to choose that option.

OTHER IMPORTANT INFORMATION
• Advance Registration Only, No Walk Ins
• Meals are included in the in person registration fees, and registration is
required to attend events during meal periods. If you choose to opt out of
a meal, note that no meal will be ordered for you.
• We are optimistic about being able to celebrate together in person.
However, should there be the unfortunate occasion of another COVID-19
surge, the District Leadership will make a decision by mid-April to pivot to
a virtual conference and notify members. If that happens, all registrations
will be changed to virtual, and the difference in the price will be refunded
to the card that was used to register.
